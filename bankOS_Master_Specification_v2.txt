7.4  Goal-based Savings & Fixed Deposits (NEW)
• Goal-based: customer sets target amount and target date. Progress bar shown on account page. Partial withdrawals blocked until goal met (configurable).

• Wallet transaction log: funding, debit, transfer, fee events

• Solidarity guarantee: any group member can be held liable for another's default (configurable per product).
• Group dashboard: total exposure, PAR, attendance rate, next meeting date.

14.  Risk & Compliance (NEW)
14.1  IFRS 9 Expected Credit Loss (ECL)
• Loan staging: Stage 1 (performing), Stage 2 (significant increase in credit risk), Stage 3 (credit-impaired)
• ECL calculation: 12-month ECL (Stage 1) and Lifetime ECL (Stage 2/3)
• Inputs: PD (Probability of Default), LGD (Loss Given Default), EAD (Exposure at Default), Discount Factor
• Provisioning journal: automatic GL postings to Loan Loss Provision accounts on EOD/EOM runs
• Scenario modelling: base / optimistic / pessimistic scenario weights configurable by tenant admin
• IFRS 9 ECL Report: stage distribution, ECL amounts, coverage ratios, period-over-period movement
• Regulatory: required by CBN for licensed MFBs. Configurable for cooperative segment.
14.2  Credit Bureau Integration
• Supported bureaus: CRC Credit Bureau, XDS (Creditinfo), FirstCentral Credit Bureau
• Pull: credit inquiry on new loan application. Returns credit score, existing facilities, delinquency history.
• Push: update bureau with new loan disbursements, repayments, and defaults (monthly batch or real-time)
• Bureau consent: customer authorises bureau pull during loan application. Consent stored with timestamp.
• Bureau report stored on loan record. Visible to Loan Officer and Compliance Officer.
14.3  Overdue Scoring & Smart Collection Routing
• Daily overdue detection job (see Section 11.7) calculates Days Past Due (DPD) per loan
• Overdue score: composite of DPD, historical repayment behaviour, loan size, customer segment
• Smart routing: high-risk accounts ? Compliance Officer queue; medium ? Loan Officer; low ? automated SMS nudge
• Escalation ladder: 1 day DPD = SMS/WhatsApp nudge; 7 DPD = Loan Officer call task; 30 DPD = formal demand letter; 90 DPD = legal team queue
• Collection log: every contact attempt recorded (channel, agent, outcome, next action)

16.  Security & UX
16.1  Security Controls
• Two-Factor Authentication: SMS OTP or TOTP Authenticator (Google/Authy). Per-user opt-in or tenant-enforced.
• Device binding: on first login from new device, user receives device verification email/SMS before access granted
• Session timeout: configurable per tenant (default 30 min idle). Session invalidated on logout.
• Brute-force protection: 5 failed login attempts ? account temporarily locked. Unlock via email link.
• Password policy: min 8 chars, must contain uppercase, lowercase, number. Expires every 90 days (configurable).
• Role-based UI: menu items and action buttons hidden/shown based on Spatie permissions
• HTTPS only. Cloudflare WAF. CSP headers. SQL injection and XSS protection via Laravel's built-in escaping.
16.2  Multi-language Support
• Primary language: English
• Additional locales (stretch goal): Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Nigerian Pidgin
• Implementation: Laravel localisation files (lang/*.json). Language switcher in user settings.
• USSD and SMS notifications: templates available in all supported languages
